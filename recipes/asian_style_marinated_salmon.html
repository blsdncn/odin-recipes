Asian Style Marinated Salmon Recipe

Description

Simple marinated salmon using the asian style sauce as a marinade.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of the asian style sauce
  • 400g Salmon filet

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F
  2. Cut 400g of salmon fillets into cubes.
  3. Prepare the asian style sauce in a medium-sized bowl
  4. Place cubed salmon into the bowl, making sure all pieces are submerged
  5. Cover top of bowl with plastic wrap and place in the fridge for 30 minutes
  6. Remove from fridge, line a baking sheet with parchment paper, and place cubed salmon, spacing out as well as possible
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes
  8. Finish for 2 minutes under the broiler on high
  9. Serve with avocado, kewpie mayo, siracha, half-cauliflower-half-white-rice, and furikake seasoning.
