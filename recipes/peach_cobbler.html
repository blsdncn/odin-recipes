Peach Cobbler Recipe

Home

Description

A (slightly) healthier peach cobbler recipe using half-allulose half-sugar, with browned butter.

Ingredients

  • 900 g sliced peaches
  • 25 g granulated sugar
  • 25 g allulose
  • 15 g lemon juice
  • 57 g salted butter
  • 125 g all-purpose flour
  • 75 g granulated sugar
  • 75 g allulose
  • 12 g baking powder
  • 1 g salt
  • 240 g whole milk
  • Optional: 5–10 g sugar (topping)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. In a 10-inch cast iron skillet, brown 57 g butter over medium heat until golden and nutty. Remove from heat.
  3. In a saucepan, combine peaches, 25 g sugar, 25 g allulose, and 15 g lemon juice. Simmer 5-7 min until soft.
  4. In a mixing bowl, whisk together flour, 75 g sugar, 75 g allulose, baking powder, and salt. Add milk and whisk until smooth.
  5. Pour batter evenly over browned butter. Do not stir.
  6. Spoon peach filling with juices evenly over batter. Do not stir.
  7. Sprinkle 5-10 g sugar on top (optional).
  8. Bake for 30-35 min, checking at 28. Tent with foil if edges darken too quickly.
  9. Let rest 10-15 min before serving to allow center to set.
