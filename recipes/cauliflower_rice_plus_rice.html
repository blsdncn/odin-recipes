Half Cauliflower Half Rice

Home

Description

Simple mix of white rice and Trader Joe's Cauliflower Rice & Veggie Stir Fry all cooked in a rice cooker. Tastes 90% like regular rice, but with some added volume.

Ingredients

  • 150g jasmine white rice
  • 100g Trader Joe's Cauliflower Rice & Veggie Stir Fry

Instructions

  1. Add 150g of rice to the rice cooker pot, rinse.
  2. Add 85% of typical water level (Cauliflower rice adds moisture, add water before cauliflower, especially if going by markings on pot instead of other measurements.)
  3. Add 100g of cauliflower rice
  4. Cook for normal amount of time and serve
