Good Asian Sauce Recipe

Description

An easy-to-make sauce good for fried rice and as a marinade.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1tbsp oyster sauce
  • 0.5-2g sesame oil to taste
  • 2-4 cloves of garlic to taste
  • 3g of grated ginger
  • 20g of honey

Instructions

  1. Chop garlic
  2. Throw everything into a small bowl
  3. Stir until honey is incorporated
  4. Enjoy.
